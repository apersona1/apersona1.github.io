Portfolio at a glance

Some screenshots and introduction to my individual game project and team project. View more introduction and demos through the links!

Adventurer(Individual Game)

An adventure game with many levels developed by my own. In the game, the player controls a character who tries to pass each level. Though simple as it seems, this game involves both skills and puzzle solving, as player has to find the correct way to pass a level with limited hints. More levels are still until development, and I plan to upload this to game platforms and app stores when it is finished.

College Life of a Programmer(Team Project)

The project made at SD Hacks in a team of four. As a life simulation game, this game adds more elements that players can interact with, such as parkour. With different stats of the character, players can encounter different scenes and toggle various events. I led this project as I put forward the idea of creating this game and wrote most of the scripts. We are still working on new functionalities.
